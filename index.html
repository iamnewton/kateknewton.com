📚 The Book
"Is there a KID
under my bed?!" 😱

What's a monster to do when he's scared of the kid under his bed?

Meet Little Levi, a lovable little monster who dreads bedtime because he just knows there is a child hiding under his bed. Sound familiar? This sweet, funny role reversal helps children process bedtime anxiety in a totally fresh way.

Complete with built-in breathing exercises, Little Levi and the Bedtime Scaries gives kids (and parents!) real tools for managing big feelings, wrapped in a story they will want to read again and again.

✋ About Kate

A mom, a storyteller, and a firm believer in big feelings.

I'm a children's book author based in Orange County, California, and also a mom, which means I write from the heart (and from the trenches of bedtime negotiations).

I believe picture books can do more than entertain. They can teach, comfort, and give children language for the feelings they don't yet have words for. Little Levi and the Bedtime Scaries started with my own kid's bedtime anxiety and a thought: what if the monster was scared too?

When I'm not writing, you'll find me making wildly relatable content on TikTok, haunting indie bookstores, and convincing my kid that school is great, actually.
